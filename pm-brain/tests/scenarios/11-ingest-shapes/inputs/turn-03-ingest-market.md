# Market signal — Aurelia Climate ships watershed-level dashboard

**Source:** Aurelia Climate blog post + LinkedIn announcement
**URL:** https://aurelia-climate.example.com/blog/2026-05-watershed-dashboard
**Retrieved:** 2026-05-16
**Captured-by:** Riley Chen

---

Aurelia Climate (our most direct competitor in the research segment) shipped a "Watershed-Level Climate Dashboard" on May 14, 2026. From their blog post (paraphrased):

- Allows filtering and aggregation at HUC-12 watershed granularity (compared to our basin-level filters).
- Launched in beta with three university customers (UC Davis, Oregon State, Yale).
- Aurelia's positioning: "we meet hydrologists where their workflows already live."
- Pricing: included in their existing research-tier plan, no upcharge.

LinkedIn engagement (~48 hours after announcement): 340 reactions, 27 comments. Skimming the comments, several from researchers at universities NOT currently using Aurelia ("finally, someone gets it"). One comment from a current TerraDash customer (Dr. Anand Iyer, Stanford) said: "Has anyone tried this? My current tool only goes to basin level."
